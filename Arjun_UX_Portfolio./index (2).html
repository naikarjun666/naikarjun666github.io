Hospitality Leadership → PM Framework

Front-of-House ≡ UI/UX Layer
Back-of-House ≡ BIT Architecture
User ≡ Valued Guest

My product management philosophy operates at the critical intersection of hospitality-driven service logic and robust business information systems. I approach product leadership believing the user is not merely a data point or persona, but a valued “guest” of the platform.

This background in hospitality leadership equips me with a unique “anticipatory service” lens — I focus not just on building features that react to current friction, but on proactively designing system architectures that minimize future execution gaps. Leveraging my MS in Business Information Technology, I ensure the internal BIT architecture (the “back of house”) perfectly synchronizes with the user-facing interface (the “front of house”), creating predictable, seamless, and delightful user journeys.

Beyond the interface, I am a collaborative orchestrator who fosters deep understanding between cross-functional teams — bridging design intent and engineering execution. Hospitality teaches you that a flaw in internal operation directly impacts the guest experience; I apply this same rigor to product management. I ensure ethical governance and clear metrics are integrated into every sprint, enabling rapid iteration without sacrificing operational stability or user trust.

“Data is the quantified voice of the guest, guiding the prioritization of the product backlog to maximize authentic impact and trust.”

Secondhand Sync

PM & UX Designer · Digital Thrifting Platform

Problem: Friction in digital thrifting discovery caused by trust, condition, and sensory gaps — requiring a transparent, reliable platform bridging physical and digital secondhand shopping.

Key Insights

  • Search ambiguity caused significant discovery friction across user segments
  • Filtering is a baseline expectation, not a differentiator
  • Two-sided market approach needed: shopper vs. admin mental models diverge sharply

Design Decisions

  • Removed global homepage search → guided discovery paths
  • Enriched metadata tags: size, condition, provenance
  • Anchored onboarding with progress bar + skip affordance

KPIs to Track

📈 Conversion Rate (view → contact seller)
🔄 30-Day Retention Rate
📉 Onboarding Drop-off Rate
⭐ SUS Findability Score

02
Observational Research Heuristic Evaluation

Whole Foods Disconnect

Sole UX Researcher · Retail Operations Research

Problem: High-tech retail systems failing employees — a large execution gap between system design and operational reality, compounding friction for both employees and customers.

Key Insights

  • Workarounds indicate system failure: paper inventory is a symptom, not a habit
  • Missing signifiers cause standoffs: butcher queue case study
  • Rigid POS tools force human error (floral department search failure)

Proposed Interventions

  • Flexible POS search: partial/fuzzy match for un-barcoded items
  • Environmental queue signifiers at deli/butcher counter
  • Visible register status indicators for shoppers

KPIs to Track

⏱ Avg. Transaction Time
❌ Cashier Error Rate
📊 Digital Inventory Compliance
💬 Employee POS Satisfaction

“User error is often system execution failure in disguise.”

Heuristic Evaluation Affordance Analysis

PM Design Manifesto

UX Evaluator · 8-Artifact Audit · Cloud Systems

Problem: Developing heuristic analysis skills for evaluating complex existing systems (AWS, Jira, Fire TV) — moving beyond building new features to critically auditing design conversational gaps at scale.

Key Insights

  • Systems prioritizing engagement over user intent break trust (Fire TV Autonomy Mandate)
  • Honest visibility is a psychological need: Dyson victory vs. Jira dropdown frustration
  • Spatial predictability is crucial for cloud UI efficiency (AWS console inconsistency)

AWS Interventions

  • Progressive Disclosure: Beginner Mode toggle for new users
  • Visual cost indicators surfaced before service launch

KPIs to Track

🔍 Heuristic violations (impact x effort)
📞 Support ticket reduction
🤝 Stakeholder UX debt alignment
📈 Beginner mode adoption rate
